Frequently Asked Questions

What are Tropes?

Tropes otherwise known as Cliches are common or overused themes or devices. One widely known example is the Chosen One trope where one character, usually the protagonist, is framed as the inevitable hero or antihero of the story, as a result of destiny, unique gifts, and/or special lineage. To learn more about the tropes listed on this site click here.

What is a Genre?

In this context a Genre is a literary composition characterized by a particular style, form, or content
