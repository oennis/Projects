Trope Definitions

What is The Chosen One?

One character, usually the protagonist, is framed as the inevitable hero or antihero of the story, as a result of destiny, unique gifts, and/or special lineage. Ex: Harry Potter

What is a Love Triangle?

A scenario or circumstance, usually depicted as a rivalry, in which two people are pursuing or involved in a romantic relationship with one person, or in which one person in a romantic relationship with someone is simultaneously pursuing or involved in a romantic relationship with someone else.

What is Friends to Lovers?

The main characters start as friends, but something in the story happens to make them fall in love.

What is Enemies to Lovers?

The main characters start as enemies or rivals, but something in the story happens to make them fall in love.

What is Love at First Sight?

Two characters meet, and fall deeply, madly, passionately in love with each other almost immediately.

What is Major Character Death?

The main character or a character of significant value to the reader is killed.

What is an Arranged Marriage?

Two characters marry one another, without actively choosing to marry each other. Instead, others make the selection and arrange the date and any other conditions that might apply to them.

What is a Slow Burn?

When the romantic attraction between characters builds slowly over the course of a novel or series.

What is Found Family?

A group of characters finds themselves united in a family bond based on shared experiences, mutual understanding, and interpersonal connection.

What is a Happy Ending?

The story ends with major problems being resolved and the main protagonist being content with the current state of things. Sometimes referred to as “happily ever after”.

What is Pregnancy?

The main character becomes pregnant, whether it be accidentally or intentionally. This can occur with the character’s romantic partner or a person they are not in a relationship with.

What is Second Chance?

Former lovers reconnect after a breakup or separation and finally find their happy ending.
